I made this poster for a non-profit business I volunteered at in 2020 called CCQuinte. We were a group of students who were raising money to support an NGO called Create Change. The mission of Create Change is to support girls in rural parts of Ghana by providing access to a higher quality of education. They also support the girls in other aspects of life. This poster was designed to spread awareness of one of the fundraising campaigns we had - making hoodies and t-shirts that reflected our brand.

I made this pattern for fun using Adobe Illustrator. It was the summer of 2020, and I had just learned how to use Adobe Illustrator a few months ago in school. I decided to go on practicing on my own so that I could have this skill in case I needed in in the future. I got the inspiration for this design from a tutorial I was trying to follow. It really helped my learn how to pay attention to detail in my designs and I learnt a lot of shortcuts around Illustrator.

I also designed this for fun using Adobe Illustrator. I started off using a tutorial and after I got the basics down, I decided to make it my own. I enjoyed creating the different colour gradients and turning what started out as just a 2D text into a 3D image. I was so impressed with this design that I had it as my lock screen for months after :)

This was another post I designed for CCQuinte. It was during the peak of the search bar design trend, and I thought I would use it and put my own spin on it. It shows the different ways people could have contributed to support out team, and I really love the background because it shows a sunset with such beautiful colours.

This is one of my favourite illustrations I have made in the past using Adobe Illustrator. Summer is my second favourite season, and I love the tropical and fun theme behind this design. I enjoyed exaggerating the fruits to have a more cartoon-y vibe and it was fun making a design where I could just hand draw most things without having to worry about precision.
